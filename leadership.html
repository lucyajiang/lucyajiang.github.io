Graduate Involvement @ Cornell CIS

At Cornell, I've gotten involved as the President of the CS Graduate Organization! Although I'm new to the department, I'm excited to work with other students to advocate for initiatives that can improve the graduate student experience. More updates to come!

Undergraduate Involvement @ UW CSE

I was involved as part of the UW Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) student chapter for three years. Our organization focused on creating a welcoming environment in the computer science department through virtual events, in-person socials, a peer mentorship program, and other initiatives. As Chair of the organization from 2020 to 2022, I led ACM in kickstarting a local fundraiser tradition, raising over $5000 for the University District Food Bank, Code.org, Black Girls CODE, and Seattle Children's Hospital combined. At graduation, I had the honor of presenting ACM Teaching Awards to three excellent faculty in the Allen School, and I also received the Service Award for my work with ACM.
